Dynamics of interacting populations and beyond

Workshop from September 9 to 12, 2025 at the Theodor-Schwartz-Haus at the Baltic Sea, Travemünde.

The main topics of the workshop are interacting populations, which can e.g. be host-pathogen interactions, or populations within a species, or interacting species or something similar.

Organizers

  • Cornelia Pokalyuk, University of Lübeck
  • Peter Pfaffelhuber, University of Freiburg

Confirmed Speaker

  • Sprecher 1 - Experte auf seinem Gebiet
  • Sprecher 2 - Langjährige Erfahrung in der Branche
  • Sprecher 3 - Innovative Ansätze und spannende Vorträge
