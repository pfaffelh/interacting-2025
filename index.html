Dynamics of interacting populations and beyond

Workshop from September 9 to 12, 2025 at the Theodor-Schwartz-Haus at the Baltic Sea, Travemünde.

The main topics of the workshop are interacting populations, which can e.g. be host-pathogen interactions, or populations within a species, or interacting species or something similar. The workshop starts on Tuesday, September 9, at 14:00, and ends on September, 12, after lunch.

If you are interested in joining the conference, please contact the organizers. If you would like to present a poster, please, mention this in your mail.

Program

The latest version of the full program including all abstracts can be found here.

Location

The Theodor-Schwartz-Haus can be reached from Lübeck (Main Station) directly by bus. Alternatively, you go by train to Travemünde Strand and than take a taxi to the Theodor-Schwartz-Haus.
